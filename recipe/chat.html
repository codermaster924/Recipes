Chat recipe

Description

Chat is a popular Indian street food that combines a variety of flavors and textures. It typically includes ingredients like boiled potatoes, chickpeas, yogurt, tamarind chutney, and various spices. The dish is known for its tangy, spicy, and sweet flavors, making it a favorite snack or appetizer.

Ingredients

  • 2 medium-sized potatoes, boiled and diced
  • 1 cup boiled chickpeas
  • 1/2 cup yogurt
  • 1/4 cup tamarind chutney
  • 1/4 cup green chutney
  • 1/2 teaspoon roasted cumin powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon red chili powder
  • Salt to taste
  • Chopped coriander leaves for garnish
  • Sev (crispy chickpea noodles) for topping

Preparation Steps

  1. In a mixing bowl, combine the boiled potatoes and chickpeas.
  2. Add yogurt, tamarind chutney, and green chutney to the mixture.
  3. Sprinkle roasted cumin powder, red chili powder, and salt. Mix well.
  4. Transfer the mixture to a serving plate.
  5. Garnish with chopped coriander leaves and top with sev.
  6. Serve immediately and enjoy your delicious chat!
